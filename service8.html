MULTIFACED SERVICES

In addition to our core services, we also offer a specialized facility equipped with a weighbridge tailored to handle heavy and oversized goods carrier vehicles. Our weighbridge plays a pivotal role in the logistics industry by providing reliable and precise weight measurements. This service is invaluable to various sectors as it furnishes industry stakeholders with accurate figures, enabling them to maintain the integrity of their goods both inbound and outbound.
Moreover, in compliance with the Central Motor Vehicles Act of 1989, we provide an essential emissions testing facility. This service ensures that vehicles meet the stipulated emissions standards necessary for roadworthiness. Following a successful emissions test, we issue a Pollution Under Control (PUC) certificate, a mandatory requirement for vehicles to legally operate on Indian roads. Our commitment to delivering accurate weight measurements and ensuring vehicles' compliance with emission standards underscores our dedication to promoting safe and environmentally responsible transportation practices.
